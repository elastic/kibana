Kibana helps you make the most of your data. Kibana is:

  • An analytics and visualization platform. Search your data for hidden insights, visualize what you’ve found in charts, maps, and more, and combine them in a dashboard.
  • A centralized hub for Elastic’s solutions. From document discovery to analyzing logs to finding security vulnerabilities, Kibana is the portal for accessing these and other capabilities.
  • A UI for managing the Elastic Stack. Manage your data and indices, monitor the health of your Elastic Stack cluster, assign user roles, and more.
